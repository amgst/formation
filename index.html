Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our certification and training services

Certification Process

The certification process typically takes 3-6 months, depending on your organization's size and readiness. This includes:

  • Initial assessment (2-3 weeks)
  • Documentation review (4-6 weeks)
  • Implementation and training (6-8 weeks)
  • Pre-audit and final certification audit (2-3 weeks)

Before starting the certification process, we recommend:

  • Review of current quality management system
  • Gap analysis against desired certification standard
  • Management commitment and resource allocation
  • Initial staff training and awareness
  • Documentation of key processes

Certification costs vary based on several factors:

  • Organization size and complexity
  • Type of certification required
  • Current system maturity
  • Geographic location

Contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific needs.

Maintenance & Support

Our post-certification support includes:

  • Regular system reviews and updates
  • Internal audit support
  • Surveillance audit preparation
  • Continuous improvement workshops
  • 24/7 technical support hotline
  • Regular newsletters and updates

Surveillance audit frequency depends on the certification:

  • ISO 9001: Annual surveillance audits
  • IATF 16949: Semi-annual surveillance audits
  • AS9100: Annual surveillance with specific requirements
  • Recertification every 3 years for most standards
